Goal Grind Assessment Quiz

Please answer the questions below. There are no right or wrong answers.

1. When you finish work or a project, how do you feel?

2. When someone praises your work, how do you react?

3. When you face a new problem or challenge, what first comes to your mind?

4. When you compare yourself to your colleagues at work, how do you feel?

5. Do you put a lot of pressure on yourself to be perfect in your work?

6. How do you deal with success?

7. What causes you the most stress in your day?

8. How do you manage stress when you feel it?

9. When you have a lot of work, what happens?

10. Do you feel that work pressures are affecting your personal life?

11. How do you feel at the end of your day?

12. Are you able to separate between work and relaxation time?

13. When you have a big project, what do you do first?

14. Can you prioritize and know what the most important thing to do is?

15. What makes you feel lost when you have a lot of work?

16. What do you do when you have close deadlines?

17. Can you stick to the plan you set for yourself?

18. When you have a big task, what do you do?

19. What makes you delay finishing your work?

20. How do you deal with a lot of tasks at the same time?

21. Can you focus on one task and finish it before starting the next?

22. What makes you leave a task unfinished?

23. Can you set deadlines for yourself and stick to them?

24. How do you organize your daily tasks?

25. When you have a difficult goal, what's the first thing you do?

26. Do you feel that the goals others set for you are suitable and achievable?

27. What makes you feel that you won't be able to achieve the goal?

28. When you achieve a difficult goal, how do you feel?

29. What do you think about the pressure that work puts on you to achieve goals?

30. Can you balance the pursuit of difficult goals with your personal life?
